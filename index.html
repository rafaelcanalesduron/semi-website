Get the insights you need and explore what’s possible

Can you make sense of your data?

Let’s admit it – If your company is like most, you are having difficulty gaining information and insights from your data. At SeMI, we believe natural language processing can give meaning and context to your data.

SeMI is a decentralized network, where members can exchange knowledge based on the insights hidden in their metadata. As a member you can receive customized and real time insights. To magnify the value you can build your own application on top of SeMI.

Enrich your knowledge and insights.

SeMI uses natural language processing (NLP) to help you make sense of your data in a way that makes sense to you. NLP focuses on the interactions between human language and computers.

SeMI links meta-data to create a decentralized semantic knowledge graph, which enables you to exponentially enrich your customer and market insights.

The power of SeMI is its ability to connect multiple systems and sources of information without a common information structure. Different systems and sources independent from each other. Everyone’s data is secure.

SeMI empowers you to make better decisions

SeMI offers two products: SeMI Local and SeMI World. To generate meaningful insights from your own data, start with SeMI Local. To dramatically enhance the quality of your knowledge and insights, go straight to SeMI World. Explore SeMI products
